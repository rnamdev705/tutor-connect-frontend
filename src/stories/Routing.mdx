# Routing

## Route protection

1. **`src/proxy.ts`** — checks `tutorconnect-token` cookie; redirects unauthenticated users from protected paths.
2. **`AppShell`** — client-side guard after hydration.

Logged-in users visiting `/login` or `/register` are redirected to `/dashboard`.
