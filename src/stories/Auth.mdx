## Route protection

`AppShell` redirects unauthenticated users to `/login`. After sign-in, users land on `/dashboard` with role-specific navigation.

## Feature-level gates

- **Parent only:** Create/edit cases, invite tutors, upload case documents.
- **Tutor only:** Browse cases, view invitations, edit tutor profile.
- **Owner check:** `canManage` — parent must own the case to edit or delete.
