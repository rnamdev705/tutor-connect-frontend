## Patterns

- **Server + client split:** Detail routes fetch data on the server; interactive views are client components.
- **Access control:** `AppShell` guards authenticated routes; feature views check role via `useAuth()`.
- **Conditionals:** `react-if` (`If`, `Then`, `Else`, `When`) instead of nested ternaries.
- **Forms:** `react-hook-form` + Zod schemas in `lib/validations/`.
- **Empty/error UI:** Shared `EmptyState` and `ErrorState` components.

## Protected route group

Authenticated pages live under `src/app/(app)/` and share `AppShell` (sidebar + top nav). Public routes: login, register, docs, unauthorized.
